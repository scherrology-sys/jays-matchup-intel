How the ball moves
Martin pitches lefties and righties almost completely differently. Against lefties, the Changeup is the primary weapon, roughly a third of his pitches. Against righties, the Slider takes over, paired with the fastball. Two different games in the same start.
The Cutter question. Martin threw a Cutter less than 2% of the time in 2024 and 2025. His first start of 2026 had the Cutter at 21%, primarily against left-handed hitters. This analysis leans heavily on what he's done across 37 starts.
