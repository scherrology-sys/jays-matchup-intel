The Slider failure
WR threw the Slider 48.8% of the time to RHH, expected 35.1%. That is a +13.7pp failure, outside the 12pp tolerance. When a pitcher's game unravels, he tends to reach for his best pitch. The Slider is WR's RHH weapon. Leaning on it more than usual is the behavioral response to being hit hard. The model knew the Slider usage was coming, it did not know WR would over-rely on it under pressure. This is exactly the kind of pattern the new research project would capture.
