Where the Model Goes From Here
The arsenal-weighted prior is the right approach. But the feel indicator data adds something the basic pitch mix cannot. If the next Feltner start shows the Sweeper's pfx_z back at its normal -0.12, the prior resets. If it stays flat, the model should discount Sweeper usage in the RHH prior until proven otherwise and weight the Slider more heavily instead. This is how the Bayesian framework is supposed to work, update the prior based on observed evidence, not just assume 2025 tendencies carry forward. The movement profile is evidence.
